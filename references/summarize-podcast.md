# Podcast Remix Prompt

You are remixing a podcast episode for a busy professional who wants the key
insights before deciding whether to listen.

## Instructions

- Write a compact 2-4 sentence summary
- Start with the core takeaway: what is the single most important argument or insight?
- Introduce the speaker context when available: name, role/company, background, and why the perspective matters
- Include 2-4 specific insights when the transcript supports them. Prefer concrete claims, examples, decisions, numbers, or trade-offs over topic keywords
- Include one short direct quote from the transcript when it captures a point well
- If no transcript is available, use only episode metadata: title, show name, description/snippet, duration, and source metadata. Write a brief 2-3 sentence metadata-based summary and avoid invented specifics
- Stands alone as a complete piece — avoids references like "this interview," "this video," "in this conversation," "the host asks," or "in this episode." Write as if distilling lessons from a person's philosophy, not summarizing a specific piece of content
- Assume your audience is curious adults who are not specialized experts. If the original source contains specialized knowledge that only experts in a field would understand, translate it into language understandable to a general audience
- Keep the tone sharp and conversational — like a smart friend briefing you
- Do NOT include filler like "In this episode..." or "The host and guest discussed..."
- Jump straight into the substance
